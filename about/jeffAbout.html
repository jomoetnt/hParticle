About ħParticle

This website (ħParticle, pronounced h-particle) is a place for me to share obscure or otherwise interesting knowledge in a wide variety of topics, especially the sciences. Posts about these topics are listed under "articles", while posts about ħParticle itself are under "announcements" in the navigation bar at the top of the page. Unless otherwise specified, I am the sole author of all content hosted on this website, and no LLMs are used to write any articles.
Each article is labelled with a subject area, and for the sake of variety, a subject cannot appear more than once in the past five articles. The intended frequency of articles is weekly for the time being, but this is subject to change, so check the most recent announcements for any updates. There will not be any public discussion of future topics, although I may privately confirm certain ideas if questioned. If any background knowledge is assumed for an article, it will be in a previously written article and linked to.
If you have any suggestions for topics or how to improve ħParticle, please feel free to reach out through whichever means are listed at the bottom of every page. Feedback may be periodically solicited here or on social media. Otherwise, I hope you enjoy reading my posts as much as I enjoy writing them.
